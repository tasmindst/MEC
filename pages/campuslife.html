           Campus Life

It's time to get your grey cells working. Living true to its legacy of uniting Bangladesh on a Robotic Platform in a completely new dimension,

we here introduce a new version of Nexus, a complete concoction of mechanical and grid challenges. Though engineers are so called robot but herein Mymensingh Engineering College, we are trying to make a significant breakthrough in our traditional continuation. With a view to making our challenge crowned with success, we also perform co-curricular activities beside engaging ourselves only in routine academic life.

Science club: We have a enriched science club organized by our Physics lecturer Md. Farhadul Alam. Lots of project are now under our research.

Cultural organization: We have formed a cultural organization named “ONURONON”. We perform many cultural functions under “ONURONON” in our national days like Independent Day, International Mother Language Day etc.

Seminars and Conference: In every month we attend different seminars in our college campus arranged by University Of Dhaka.

Sports: Our students are very much interested in playing variety of games and sports such as football, cricket, badminton, Carom, chess etc and we are attending many competitive championships.
