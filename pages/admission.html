Admission Qualifications:

  • Applicants must be SSC and HSC passed having total GPA 8.OO. Though applicants must have GPA-3.50 in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry separately. They also require a minimum GPA of 3.00 in English.
  • In case of GCE ‘0’ level and GCE ‘A’ level an average grade of ‘B’ is required in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English. Though students of GCE ‘A’ level must require ‘A’ grade in minimum two subjects among Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and GRADE-B in other subjects.
  • Those students who have passed HSC or GCE 'A' Level before 2010 are not eligible for application.

Examination System:

  • The eligible students will have to attend an examination on 100 marks (Mathematics-30, Physics-30, Chemistry-20, English-20) and the left 100 marks will be count from SSC (40 marks) and HSC (6O marks).The merit list will be made on the basis of these 200 marks. About 60 students will be selected for admission after health test.

Deadlines:

  • Application Duration:NOVEMBER 20, 2011 to DECEMBER 11, 2011 ( Office Time)
  • List of eligible candidates publish: DECEMBER 15, 2011.
  • Admit card distribution: DECEMBER 15, 2011(BY POST)
  • Admission test date & time: DECEMBER 23, 2011
