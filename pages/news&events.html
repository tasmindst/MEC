Engineering Analysis and Futuristic Design Conference- 2011

A conference arranged by the students of department of EEE is going to be held on 15 May 2011. This is about the recent advances of engineering sectors and different aspects of engineering analysis. Dr. Ratan Kumar Nandi, the chairman of the conference will declare the grand opening of the conference.

National Victory Day Celebration

All the students and the teachers with the staffs has celebrated the National Victory Day with the proper respect. Students arranged a rally at the mid night on 12.00 am. The day was started with the flag rising and the national anthem. Then, the short cultural programme and discuss about the significance of the victory day. A badminton tournament named “ Bijoy Dibosh Badminton Tournament” was the most attractive programme of the day. Students has celebrated the day very much, but due to the examination of the first semester examination, the programme was a little bit short.
